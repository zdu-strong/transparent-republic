**National Reserve Fund**

The nation’s finances shall develop on the foundation of a reserve fund. The President shall set a long-term reserve target. When the reserve exceeds this target, the surplus shall be distributed to citizens through sovereign dividends. When the reserve falls below this target, government spending shall be reduced and taxes shall be increased in order to maintain fiscal stability.

The funds allocated to each department, the funds allocated to each state, and any emergency borrowing shall all be public and transparent.
