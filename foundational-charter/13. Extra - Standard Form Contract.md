The government provides **Standardized Form Contracts**, such as employment contracts and loan contracts.

The government does not restrict loan interest rates or compound interest, but the enforcement of loan contracts belongs to the government.

If the borrower fails to repay the loan, the creditor may file a lawsuit in court. The court will then carry out bankruptcy liquidation and transfer the proceeds from the auction to the creditor.

There is no legal method of private debt collection. For example, debt collection phone calls would be regarded as harassment and punished according to the law.
