1. Fine<br/>
For acts such as littering, a monetary fine shall be imposed.<br/>
If the fine is not paid within three months, bankruptcy liquidation procedures shall be initiated.<br/>

2. Bankruptcy Liquidation<br/>
If, after bankruptcy liquidation, the individual is still unable to fully repay the fine, a Public Restricted Area Penalty shall be imposed.<br/>

3. Public Restricted Area Penalty<br/>
A designated sheltered area shall be established. The individual shall wear electronic ankle monitoring and remain in the restricted area for at least four hours per day, for multiple consecutive days.<br/>
If the individual still refuses to accept the Public Restricted Area Penalty within the prescribed period, imprisonment shall be enforced.<br/>
