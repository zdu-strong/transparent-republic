# Land Auction

The government will list all land that can enter the market on a land auction website, with an initial price of 0.<br/>

Starting from the first bid, the auction will end after 3 days, and the highest bidder will obtain the land.<br/>

If a bidder wins the auction but fails to pay for the purchase, they will be sued in court.<br/>

Anyone may place a bid and may cancel their bid at any time.<br/>

If multiple bidders place the same bid, the auction system will rank them by randomly assigned bidding numbers to determine the buyer.<br/>
