# Patrol Police

Police are divided into **patrol police** and **court police**.<br/>

**Patrol police** are the police of a governed area. They are responsible for protecting regional safety and traffic safety, and for helping the people. However, they have no law-enforcement authority. They may only file a lawsuit with the court, and the court will conduct the trial.<br/>

**Court police** are police officers dispatched by the court. They are responsible for carrying out specific orders issued by the court.<br/>

Police may ask citizens to present their ID without explaining the reason to the citizens, and citizens have the obligation to present their ID.<br/>

Every time police ask a citizen to present their ID, they must later add the reason for doing so into the system, so that officials can review it and decide whether to continue employing that police officer.<br/>
