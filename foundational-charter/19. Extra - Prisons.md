# Prisons

## Prisons are divided into high-security areas and low-security areas.<br/>

### Low-security areas

Prisoners in low-security areas may choose each day to use prisoner-only facilities such as libraries, basketball courts, football fields, and other activity spaces.<br/>

### High-security areas

Prisoners in high-security areas may be transferred to low-security areas if they commit no violations for 3 months.<br/>

If a prisoner threatens another person, the case shall be tried by a court. The prisoner shall receive an additional 1 month of imprisonment and shall be assigned 1 dedicated prison officer for supervision.<br/>

If a prisoner attempts to harm another person, the case shall be tried by a court. The prisoner shall receive an additional 3 months of imprisonment and shall be assigned 3 dedicated prison officers for supervision.<br/>

If a prisoner harms another person, the case shall be tried by a court. The prisoner shall receive an additional 6 months of imprisonment and shall be assigned 6 dedicated prison officers for supervision.<br/>

The number of dedicated prison officers assigned to supervise a prisoner shall decrease by 1 each month. When the number of assigned prison officers is greater than or equal to 2, the prisoner is prohibited from approaching other prisoners. When the number of assigned prison officers is 1, the prisoner may communicate with other prisoners under prison officer, and the prison officer shall stop the prisoner from using threatening language.<br/>

## Special criminal provisions for offenses committed by prisoners inside prisons

The law shall establish special criminal provisions for offenses committed by prisoners inside prisons.<br/>

If a prisoner threatens or harms another person, the case shall be tried by a court. The prisoner shall receive an increased sentence and be transferred to a high-security areas.<br/>
