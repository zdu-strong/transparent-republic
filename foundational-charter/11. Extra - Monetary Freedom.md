# Monetary Freedom

The state shall not investigate the deposits of individuals or companies, nor shall it investigate how money flows, except for the supervision of the total cash reserves held by commercial banks.<br/>
The state does not operate banks and is not responsible for currency exchange. Deposits, withdrawals, and currency exchange shall be handled by commercial banks or third-party platforms.<br/>
Lumen is a free, open currency exchange system with floating exchange rates determined by contributions and market dynamics.<br/>
Transparent Republic will support Lumen as a free currency platform. Lumen has not been open-sourced yet and is still under development.<br/>
