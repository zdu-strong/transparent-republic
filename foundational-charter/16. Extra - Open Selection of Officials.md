
# Open Selection of Officials

An Open Selection Department shall be established. It is responsible for defining the positions available for selection and the eligibility requirements for candidates.<br/>

Candidates may then register. Those who meet the requirements will enter the open selection process conducted by Community Supervisors.<br/>

After the open selection process is completed, the National Supervisory Council will communicate with and vote on the top four candidates.<br/>

In the open selection process, the system randomly divides candidates into groups of four. Each Community Supervisor votes on one group of candidates. A Community Supervisor may vote for multiple candidates at the same time, or may choose not to vote.<br/>

Candidates are then ranked according to the total amount of weighted public mandate they receive.<br/>
